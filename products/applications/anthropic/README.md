## Cover letter draft

**Role:** Research Scientist, Life Sciences

Dear Anthropic Hiring Team,

I am applying for the Research Scientist, Life Sciences role in San Francisco. I bring a PhD in Materials Science and Engineering, peer-reviewed publications, extensive laboratory experience across analytical and biological methods, and a career spent deploying complex technical systems at global scale.

My research foundation includes nano-catalyst development for syngas-to-alcohol conversion, with publications in *Fuel* and the *Journal of Renewable and Sustainable Energy*. I have hands-on experience with NMR, GC-MS, SEM, TEM, HPLC, PCR, and related instrumentation. Before my energy career, I worked in an FDA-regulated nucleic acid testing laboratory at the American Red Cross, handling high-volume biohazardous samples for HIV, Hepatitis C, and West Nile Virus testing.

Beyond the lab, I led engineering at ALL Power Labs (1,000+ deployments in 35 countries), was Principal Investigator on an NSF grant, won the 2018 Water Abundance XPrize, and published the 2024 Energy Plan for the Alaska Native Village of Eklutna. This combination of rigorous experimental science and real-world deployment is what I hope to bring to Anthropic's life sciences work.

I am based in the Bay Area and eager to contribute to AI systems developed with scientific rigor and safety at their core.

Sincerely,  
Dr. Julia Hasty  
jay@resilientmarkets.com  
https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-jay/
