## Michael Christian's (~Dec 2016–Mar 2017)

- **Michael Christian** is a real, well-known Oakland-based large-scale sculptor, active in Burning Man circles since 1996 (20-time Burning Man arts grant recipient, apprenticed under sculptor Luis Jimenez, moved to the Bay Area in 1995). Sources: [About – Michael Christian](https://www.michaelchristian.com/about/), [Oakland Originals — Michael Christian](https://oaklandoriginals.com/meet/michael_christian/).
- **Jay was a paying tenant on his property**, ~$885/month (confirmed via a Dec 2016 email to mc@michaelchristian.com and co-tenant "Liz Judkins": *"Im understanding that I need to get you, Michael, $885 before Jan 1st... Can you remind me what the best way would be to pay you?"* Michael's reply: *"pay pal is fine... actually prefer you paypal after the first so will go on 2017 income."*).
- Living quarters appear informal/industrial (a Feb 2017 email from co-tenant Liz Judkins mentions a plastic-sheeted skylight needing a fix for heating).
- **Also a professional connection**: Michael Christian was a fellow shareholder in ALL Power Labs (per a 2015 stockholder consent document) — meaning this wasn't just a landlord relationship, it overlapped with her APL-era professional network (see `../all-power-labs/README.md`). Separate threads show her buying/borrowing steel stock from him and coordinating truck moves, consistent with a live/work shop relationship.
- No lease or photos found in Drive beyond the payment-coordination emails.

## Flava Packet (~2015–2016)

- A real, recurring party/community house in Jay's social circle — referenced casually across 2015-2016 emails:
  - Nov 2015: *"There is a black label party at the flava packet on Sat night"*; a friend's reply confirms a band ("the NERV") was playing there.
  - Dec 2016, Jay's own invite email ("You're Invited! Sagittarius bday party in Outer Space!"): *"come back to Flava and party/potluck/fire barrel it up tomorrow/Saturday!"*
  - July 2016: a friend (Gui Cavalcanti) texts *"I'm at Flava... I'll go home and meet you there"* — indicating Flava Packet was his residence too, i.e., a shared house Jay was closely tied to.
  - A 2016 Burning Man packing-list document ("Jay and Sara Burn 2016") repeatedly tags items as sourced "(flava)" — hair dye, headlamp, soap, extension cords, hammock, night lights — strongly suggesting **Jay stored personal belongings and effectively lived/stayed at Flava Packet** during this period, using it as a home base.
- No lease, rent, or address documents found; no public web presence (expected for an informal house).

## Finn House (~2023–2024, ongoing)
