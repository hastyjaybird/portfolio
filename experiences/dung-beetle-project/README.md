## Important correction (2026-07-04, per Jay) — TWO COMPLETELY UNRELATED "DUNG BEETLE" THINGS

**There are two entirely separate "Dung Beetle" things, and they must never be conflated:**
1. **A character Jay played** as part of the **Swarm** theme camp at Big Stick Shindig (Take 3 Presents, 2024, "Primordial Soup" theme) — a costume/performance bit fitting the insect-carnival concept. See `../take-3-presents/README.md`.
2. **The Dung Beetle Project (this folder)** — a real AfrikaBurn engineering installation: a waste-processing power system, built by **Jeff Barbee under Alliance Earth**, that **ran power for the camp and DJ booth** in South Africa.
