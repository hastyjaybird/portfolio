## Training that preceded this role (confirmed 2026-07-07)

- **Portland Community College — Solar Installer and Technician's Training Course, completed June 2008, Portland, Oregon.** Confirmed directly on Jay's own 2018 CV ("Jay_CV (1).pdf," Drive), listed under both Education and Certifications ("Solar Installers Technician's Certification").
- **What the course actually was (public record):** PCC's Community Education "Solar, Thermal and Electric Systems Installer" series — an 8-week hands-on course taught by John Patterson, founder of the Portland solar company **Mr. Sun Solar** (in business since the early 1980s; Patterson has taught this PCC course since 1995). It's approved for 20 hours by Oregon's State Renewable Energy Apprenticeship Committee and covers solar thermal (water/pool heating) and solar PV systems, code compliance, grid-tie basics, hands-on roof-mounting practice, and business topics (customer evaluation, tax credits). **Not a NABCEP credential** — it's a continuing-education/apprenticeship-hours course, not a nationally accredited installer certification, so don't overstate it as "NABCEP certified."
- **Timeline:** June 2008 completion sits right at the tail end of the Windward Education & Research Center role (May 2007–June 2008) and just before the SunBurst Solar installer job (2009–2010) — a coherent sequence: Red Cross lab work → Windward (including PV/sustainable-systems work) overlapping into this PCC course → the SunBurst Solar installer role that put it into practice.
- **Recommended accurate phrasing for resume/bio use:** "Completed Portland Community College's Solar, Thermal and Electric Systems Installer training (taught by Mr. Sun Solar's founder), June 2008."
